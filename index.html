Gold Clash

NOTE: Numbers and mechanics may have changed!

Gather 9999 gold to win.

Player with most gold has a crown above his/her head (and on minimap) and will have it's movement slowed.

Dying will remove gold (-35%) from the player.

A kill grants gold (+500).

Treasure chest monster in the middle of the map that drops gold when attacked.

Every 1 minute, player will receieve a gold interest (+15%).

Every 2 minute, part of the map will fall.

The shop will let the player buy artifacts to boost stats during the game.

Everyime you level up, you will be able to select 1 of 3 random passive abilities.

Glitch Dungeon

NOTE: Numbers and mechanics may have changed!

The rogue-like dungeons contains puzzle, bosses, items, store/upgrade NPC and more.

Player may be able to select pet at start that gives a passive buff.

Player can select weapons that changes the active weapon skill.

Boss Raid

NOTE: Numbers and mechanics may have changed!

You will get 5 random abilities and select which to re-roll with a total of 5 re-rolls.

5 different weapons is available to select. They have different elements and skills.

There is a time limit on the boss raid. (Black Alice seems to be 07:30)

There will be a damage ranking showing all players damage on the boss.

If you die, you will be spectating the fight from a high angle shot.
